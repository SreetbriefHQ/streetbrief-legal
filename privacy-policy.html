Privacy Policy — StreetBrief (@StreetBriefHQ)

Last updated: June 30, 2026

StreetBrief ("we", "us") publishes daily US stock market recap content on YouTube, TikTok, and Instagram (the "Channels"). This policy explains what data we collect and how it is used in connection with the Channels and any associated apps used to publish content to them.

Information we collect

  • Platform account data: When you follow/subscribe to the Channels, the relevant platform (YouTube/Google, TikTok, Meta) handles your account data under its own privacy policy. We do not separately collect or store your platform account data.
  • API access tokens: To publish content automatically, we use official platform APIs (YouTube Data API, TikTok Content Posting API, Instagram Graph API). These APIs issue access tokens tied to the StreetBrief account itself, not to viewers. We store these tokens securely and use them solely to publish StreetBrief own content.
  • Audience engagement data: We may view aggregate, anonymized analytics (views, likes, comments) provided by each platform creator/studio dashboard to understand how content performs. We do not collect personal data from individual viewers beyond what the platform itself provides in aggregate.

How we use information

  • To publish daily market recap videos, short clips, and related posts to the Channels.
  • To understand aggregate content performance and improve future episodes.

What we do not do

  • We do not sell or share personal data with third parties.
  • We do not use viewer data for advertising outside of the platforms own native ad systems.
  • We do not collect financial account information, trading data, or any personal financial details from viewers.

Content disclaimer

StreetBrief content is for informational and educational purposes only and is not financial advice.
